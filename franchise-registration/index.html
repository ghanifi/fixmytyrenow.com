Fix My Tyre Now Franchise Registration

Join a Growing Mobile Tyre Fitting Network in the UK

Fix My Tyre Now offers a powerful franchise opportunity for businesses and individuals looking to grow in the mobile tyre fitting industry. We generate customers, manage bookings, and send you ready-to-go jobs so you can focus on delivering the service.

How the Franchise Works

Our system is based on postcode territories. As a franchise partner, you can purchase and operate in as many postcode areas as you like. There is no limit to your expansion.

  • You can own multiple postcode areas
  • You pay franchise fees per postcode
  • You can scale your business anytime
  • You receive jobs based on your selected territories

Postcode Territory System

Each franchise partner operates within chosen postcode areas. This ensures clear coverage, better lead targeting, and higher conversion rates.

Monthly Franchise Model

Franchise payments are made monthly and continue for a minimum of 12 months. As long as your payments remain active, Fix My Tyre Now will continue sending you new customer jobs.

Guaranteed Jobs with Reduced Risk

We focus on quality leads, not just quantity.

  • Customers receive pricing before booking
  • Deposits are collected in advance
  • Jobs are confirmed and ready
  • Cancellation risk is minimised

This allows you to focus on completing jobs and generating revenue instead of chasing unreliable leads.

What You Get as a Franchise Partner

Full Support

  • Ongoing operational support
  • Direct communication when needed
  • Proven business processes

Marketing & Advertising

  • Continuous digital marketing investment
  • Local SEO and Google visibility
  • Lead generation campaigns in your postcode areas

Technology

  • Smart booking system
  • Automated job distribution
  • Customer management tools

Training

  • Onboarding guidance
  • Booking and customer handling training
  • Best practices for increasing earnings

High Quality Standards

We maintain high service standards across all franchise partners. This ensures customer satisfaction, repeat business, and strong brand reputation in every location.

Frequently Asked Questions

How many postcode areas can I buy?

You can purchase and operate in as many postcode areas as you like. There is no limit, allowing you to scale your business freely.

How does the payment structure work?

Franchise fees are charged monthly per postcode area. Payments continue for a minimum of 12 months.

Will I continue receiving jobs every month?

Yes. As long as your franchise payments remain active, you will continue to receive new customer jobs from Fix My Tyre Now.

Are the jobs confirmed?

Yes. Customers receive pricing in advance and deposits are collected, meaning jobs are confirmed and ready to complete.

What happens if a job is cancelled?

Cancellation risk is kept to a minimum due to deposit collection and pre-confirmation of bookings.

Do I get support after joining?

Yes. All franchise partners receive ongoing support, training, and access to our systems and processes.

Do I need experience in tyre fitting?

Experience is recommended, but we provide onboarding guidance and support to help you get started.

How do I start?

Simply fill out the registration form on this page, choose your postcode areas, and our team will contact you with the next steps.

Frequently Asked Questions

Which London boroughs do FixMyTyreNow cover?

We cover all 32 London boroughs — from Barnet and Enfield in the north to Bromley and Croydon in the south, Havering and Redbridge in the east to Hillingdon and Hounslow in the west. Select your borough above to see services and pricing for your area.

How quickly can you get to me in London?

Our average arrival time is under 20 minutes across Greater London. Once you book, we dispatch the nearest available fitter to your exact location — whether that's your driveway, a car park, or the roadside.

Do you offer same-day mobile tyre fitting in London?

Yes. Same-day and emergency tyre fitting is available 24 hours a day, 7 days a week across every London borough. You can book online in under 60 seconds with just a £10 holding deposit — the remainder is paid to the fitter on the day.

What tyre services do you provide across London?

We offer emergency tyre replacement, standard tyre fitting, puncture repair, wheel balancing, and run-flat tyre replacement. All services are performed at your chosen location — no garage visit required.

Do I need to go to a garage for tyre fitting in London?

No. FixMyTyreNow is a fully mobile service. Our fitters bring all the equipment needed and complete the job at your home, workplace, or roadside. There is never any need to drive to or wait at a tyre centre.

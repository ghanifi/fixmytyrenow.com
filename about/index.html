About FixMyTyreNow

Who We Are

FixMyTyreNow was founded by Harry McKnight (IT specialist) and Arthur Smith (automotive technician). They combined their experience in technology and vehicle repair to build a service that is simple, fast, and reliable.

The idea came from seeing how slow and inconvenient traditional tyre services can be, especially when drivers are stuck and need immediate help. The business was built around a technology-first approach: using data and real usage patterns, technicians are positioned in the areas where demand is highest, allowing faster response times across all 32 London boroughs.

The booking system is designed to work instantly. When you place a booking, it detects your location and assigns the closest available technician. Whether you are at home, at work, or on the roadside, help is dispatched without delay. In most cases, a technician can reach you within 20 minutes.

The goal has always been clear: provide a fast, dependable service without the hassle of visiting a garage, while keeping the process straightforward for the customer.

Our team consists of experienced tyre technicians who have worked across roadside assistance, garages, and fleet services. We combined that real-world experience with a smarter, more efficient service model that eliminates the need for towing, waiting rooms, or unnecessary delays.

We provide a complete range of mobile tyre services across London, available when and where you need them.

Whether you are stuck on the roadside or simply prefer the convenience of having tyres fitted at your home or office, we bring the solution directly to you.

Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly can you reach me?

Our response time depends on your location and traffic conditions, with an average arrival time of 20 minutes across all 32 London boroughs.

Do I need to know my tyre size?

No. If you are unsure, our team can help identify the correct tyre based on your vehicle.

Can you repair any puncture?

Not all punctures can be safely repaired. If the damage is within repairable limits, we will fix it. Otherwise, we will recommend a replacement for your safety.

Do you offer emergency services?

Yes. We provide emergency tyre replacement and roadside assistance across London.

What areas do you cover?

We cover all areas within Greater London, including central, north, east, west, and south regions.

Do you work with businesses?

Yes. We offer both one-time services and subscription-based plans for businesses and fleet operators.

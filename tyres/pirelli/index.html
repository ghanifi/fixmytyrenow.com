Why Choose Pirelli Tyres?

Pirelli is one of Italy's most iconic manufacturing brands and the world's only tyre company exclusively focused on the premium and prestige market. Pirelli tyres are synonymous with high performance, combining Formula 1 technical heritage with road tyre engineering to produce some of the highest-rated performance tyres available. The brand is the official Formula 1 tyre supplier and holds OEM fitment contracts with manufacturers including Ferrari, Lamborghini, Porsche, BMW, and Maserati.

Beyond performance vehicles, Pirelli also produces the Cinturato and Scorpion ranges to serve everyday passenger cars, all-season drivers, and SUV owners. FixMyTyreNow supplies and fits Pirelli tyres across all 32 London boroughs, with mobile fitting at your home, workplace, or roadside.

Popular Pirelli Tyre Models

Pirelli P Zero

The Pirelli P Zero is one of the world's most recognisable performance tyres. Engineered for ultra-high-performance sports cars and luxury saloons, it delivers exceptional dry and wet grip, precise steering response, and high-speed stability. It is available in OEM-specific variants marked with ★ (Star for BMW), N0/N1 (Porsche), MO (Mercedes), and PZ (Porsche) designations — ensuring the exact compound and construction specified by the vehicle manufacturer.

Pirelli Cinturato P7

The Cinturato P7 is Pirelli's premium all-round touring tyre for passenger cars. It combines a comfortable ride with responsive handling, low rolling resistance, and strong wet braking performance. The Cinturato P7 Blue variant is specifically optimised for fuel efficiency, carrying an A EU label rolling resistance rating in many sizes.

Pirelli Cinturato All Season SF2

The Cinturato All Season SF2 provides genuine year-round capability for passenger car drivers who want to avoid seasonal tyre changes. It holds the 3-Peak Mountain Snowflake certification for winter use and delivers competitive wet and dry performance across all seasons. A strong choice for London drivers who occasionally travel to colder regions.

Pirelli Scorpion Range

The Scorpion series is Pirelli's dedicated SUV, crossover, and 4x4 tyre family. The Scorpion Verde is an all-season option optimised for lower rolling resistance and eco-conscious driving. The Scorpion Zero Asimmetrico prioritises performance grip for sports SUVs. The Scorpion Winter delivers winter-specific traction in cold and snowy conditions. All three are available across common SUV sizes.

Pirelli Tyres London: Frequently Asked Questions

Do you supply Pirelli tyres?

Yes. We stock Pirelli tyres for performance cars, SUVs, and standard passenger vehicles. Key lines include Pirelli P Zero (high-performance), Cinturato P7 (touring), Cinturato All Season SF2, and the Scorpion series for SUVs and 4x4s.

Are Pirelli P Zero tyres available for sports cars?

Yes. We carry P Zero in a wide range of sizes covering popular sports cars including Porsche, Ferrari, BMW M-series, and Audi RS models. Some sizes are marked as ★ (Star), N0, N1, or MO variants — specify your vehicle at booking to ensure we bring the correct OEM-approved fitment.

What is the Pirelli Scorpion range?

The Pirelli Scorpion series is designed for SUVs, crossovers, and 4x4 vehicles. The Scorpion Verde is an all-season option optimised for lower rolling resistance. The Scorpion Zero Asimmetrico prioritises performance grip. The Scorpion Winter delivers winter-specific traction. We stock all three across common SUV sizes.

Can Pirelli tyres be fitted at home?

Yes. FixMyTyreNow provides mobile Pirelli tyre fitting across all 32 London boroughs. We come to your home, office, or roadside and complete the fitting with professional balancing on site.

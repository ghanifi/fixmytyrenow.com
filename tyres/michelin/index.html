Why Choose Michelin Tyres?

Michelin is one of the world's most respected tyre manufacturers, with a reputation built on industry-leading wet grip, long tread life, and consistent performance across all weather conditions. Independent test data repeatedly places Michelin at or near the top for wet braking and rolling resistance — two of the most safety-critical tyre attributes for UK roads.

Michelin tyres are original equipment on many premium vehicles, including those from BMW, Mercedes-Benz, Audi, Porsche, and Renault. Whether you drive a family hatchback, an SUV, or a high-performance sports car, Michelin offers a model suited to your vehicle and driving style. FixMyTyreNow supplies and fits the full Michelin range across all 32 London boroughs, with no garage visit required.

Popular Michelin Tyre Models

Michelin Pilot Sport 5

The Michelin Pilot Sport 5 is a high-performance summer tyre engineered for sports cars, hot hatches, and performance-focused drivers. It delivers exceptional dry handling, short braking distances, and outstanding wet grip — earning top ratings in Euro NCAP tyre tests. Available in a wide range of low-profile sizes for performance vehicles.

Michelin Primacy 4+

The Primacy 4+ is Michelin's flagship comfort and safety tyre for everyday passenger cars. It prioritises wet braking performance and retains its safety characteristics even as the tread wears, thanks to Michelin's EverGrip technology. Ideal for family saloons, hatchbacks, and MPVs used on UK roads year-round.

Michelin CrossClimate 2

The CrossClimate 2 is Michelin's premium all-season tyre, carrying the 3-Peak Mountain Snowflake (3PMSF) winter certification while delivering summer-level dry and wet performance. It is one of the highest-rated all-season tyres in the UK market and an excellent choice for drivers who want year-round capability without seasonal changes.

Michelin Energy Saver+

The Energy Saver+ is engineered for fuel efficiency and low rolling resistance, making it well suited to urban commuters and drivers looking to reduce fuel costs. It carries an A or B EU label rating for rolling resistance and maintains safe wet stopping distances throughout its tyre life.

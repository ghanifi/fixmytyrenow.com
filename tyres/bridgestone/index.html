Why Choose Bridgestone Tyres?

Bridgestone is the world's largest tyre manufacturer by revenue, producing tyres for passenger cars, SUVs, motorcycles, aircraft, and heavy industrial vehicles. The brand is headquartered in Japan and has an international reputation for quality, durability, and technology. Bridgestone tyres are OEM fitments on vehicles from Toyota, Lexus, Honda, and various European manufacturers, including certain BMW and Mercedes-Benz models.

Bridgestone invests heavily in wet performance and longevity research, and its passenger car range covers a broad spectrum from premium performance (Potenza) through comfortable touring (Turanza) to rugged SUV duty (Dueler). FixMyTyreNow supplies and fits the full Bridgestone range across all 32 London boroughs, with mobile fitting that comes to your location — no garage visit required.

Popular Bridgestone Tyre Models

Bridgestone Potenza Sport

The Potenza Sport is Bridgestone's flagship summer performance tyre, designed for drivers who prioritise lateral grip, dry handling precision, and short wet braking distances. It is suited to sports saloons, coupes, and hot hatches and carries strong ratings in independent tyre tests for high-performance categories. Available in a wide range of low-profile sizes for performance vehicles.

Bridgestone Turanza T005

The Turanza T005 is Bridgestone's all-round touring tyre for everyday passenger cars, balancing ride comfort, wet safety, and longevity. It is among the best-reviewed premium touring tyres for UK roads and is particularly effective in wet and mixed conditions typical of British weather. Suited to family hatchbacks, saloons, and MPVs.

Bridgestone Turanza All Season 6

The Turanza All Season 6 brings Bridgestone's touring tyre expertise to the all-season market, providing year-round capability with the 3-Peak Mountain Snowflake certification for winter use. It is well suited to London drivers who want a single tyre for all weather conditions without compromising on comfort or wet performance.

Bridgestone Dueler Range

The Bridgestone Dueler range is engineered for SUVs, crossovers, and 4x4 vehicles. The Dueler H/P Sport delivers performance SUV handling with strong wet grip. The Dueler H/T (Highway Terrain) prioritises comfort and durability for road-biased SUVs. The Dueler A/T (All Terrain) extends capability to light off-road use. All Dueler variants are stocked in common SUV sizes.

Bridgestone Tyres London: Frequently Asked Questions

Do you supply Bridgestone tyres?

Yes. We stock Bridgestone tyres for passenger cars, performance vehicles, and SUVs. We carry the Bridgestone Potenza Sport (high-performance), Turanza T005 (comfort), Turanza All Season 6, and Dueler range for SUVs.

What is the difference between Bridgestone Potenza and Turanza?

The Bridgestone Potenza Sport is a summer performance tyre offering high lateral grip and precise steering response for spirited drivers. The Turanza T005 is an all-round touring tyre prioritising comfort, wet safety, and longevity for everyday driving. The Turanza All Season 6 adds year-round capability.

Are Bridgestone tyres original equipment on any vehicles?

Yes. Bridgestone is an OEM tyre supplier for Toyota, Lexus, Honda, and various European manufacturers. Some models come from the factory with Bridgestone Turanza or Potenza tyres. Replacing like-for-like maintains the original handling balance.
